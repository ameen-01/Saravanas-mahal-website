About The Venue

A Kalyana Mandapam Built For Comfort, Not Just Ceremony

Saravanas Mahal is a banquet and conference hall in Old Pallavaram, Chennai, with a single well-designed event floor that seats 250 guests comfortably and stretches to 500 in a floating arrangement for receptions. The hall stays air-conditioned through the day, backed by power generators so the lights, sound and kitchens never pause mid-event.

Couples and families get a dedicated bridal room to prepare in, on-site parking for cars and two-wheelers, and the freedom to bring in their own decorators, DJs and caterers.

  • Seats up to 250 guests, 500 in floating layout
  • Fully air-conditioned hall and guest rooms
  • On-site parking for 8+ cars and 30+ two-wheelers
  • Private bridal room & power backup
  • Outside decorators, DJs and caterers welcome
